Demitris G. Anastasiou

A short introduction about me.

Rural & Surveying Engineer. I was awarded as Doctor of Engineering of NTUA in July 2017. Since 2010, I worked on nine research and public projects, on design and implementation of geodetic studies and the management of geospatial data. I have experience to work independently in the development and maintenance of GeoDatabases, Geoserver and ’Big Data’ management. In the recent research project ’SEISMO’ I was responsible for the development of GeoPlatform to manage all the available geodetic and spatial data/meta-data. Also I have specialized in the analysis of terrestrial and satellite geodetic data for high precision studies. I have strong background in using as administrator CAD Softwares and GIS Packages. Finally, I participated in scientific journal articles, international conference proceeding and technical reports.
Specialized: Geodesy; GNSS Precise Processing; Database; Spatial Data Management;
